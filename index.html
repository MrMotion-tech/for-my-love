For You ❤️

Happy 18th, my love ❤️🎂

I’ve been thinking about this for a while…
Trying to find the right words for you
But the truth is You’re the kind of person
Words never fully capture

Because how do I explain
What you’ve become to me
In just three months?

Three months…
Sounds small to anyone else
But to me?
It feels like something bigger than time

Like every day with you
Carries more meaning than it should
Like every conversation
Leaves a mark I can’t ignore

You didn’t just come into my life
You settled in it
Softly… naturally…
Like you were always meant to be there

And yeah… we joke, we laugh
We have our moments
Even the ones that don’t make sense to anyone else

Like “Our Song” *don't tell me you forgot lol*
That little thing between us
Might sound random to the world
But to me?
It’s one of those small pieces
That make us… us

And I love that
I love how we have our own world
Our own language
Our own meaning in things

That’s how I know this is real

Because it’s not just the big moments
It’s the small ones
The ones nobody sees
That matter the most to me

And today…
You turn 18

A whole new chapter of your life
A new version of you
Stronger, wiser, even more beautiful

And I just sit here thinking…
Damn
I really got someone like you

Someone with a soft heart
Someone who loves God
Someone who cares deeply
Even when it’s hard

And I won’t lie…
It hurts a little today

Knowing I’m not there with you
Not able to hold you
Not able to look you in your eyes
And tell you all of this in person

I wish I could be there, mama
I really do

I wish I could show you
Not just say it
Show you how much you mean to me

The way I’d look at you
The way I’d hold your hand a little tighter
Like I don’t ever want to lose you

Because that’s the truth

I don’t just like you
I don’t just want you for now

We’ve only been together for three months…
But I wanna be with you
Till I can’t even keep count anymore

Till months turn into years
And years stop being something we measure

I want the kind of love
That doesn’t fade with time
But grows with it

The kind where
Even on random days
You still feel how much I care

So on your birthday…

I don’t just wish you happiness
I don’t just wish you love

I promise you something

That as long as I’m here
You won’t ever have to question
What you mean to me

Because you’re not just someone I’m with

You’re someone I’m choosing
Again and again
Every single day

Happy 18th, my girl ❤️

This is just the beginning…
One of many birthdays
One of many memories

And one day?
I won’t be wishing I was there

I will be there

With you

Always

My Promise

I promise to choose you
Again and again
Every single day

I promise to love you louder
Hold you closer
And never make you question your place in my life

Till I can’t keep count anymore ❤️

I LOVE YOU ❤️

I wish I was there with you today mama… but this is from my heart
